Chartered Accountant<\/p>\n

Emmanuel Kanabe is CEO & Principal Consultant of iGeneration Consulting and Founder and Board Advisor of Aviation Partners Africa. Manny is a chartered Accountant (ACMA, CGMA), a member of the Chartered Management Institute, UK (MCMI) and has an MBA from Kingston Business School, UK.<\/p>\n

Director Of Administration<\/p>\n

Marilyn Omuabor, a graduate of Linguistics with years of administrative experience garnered from both the public and private sector in Nigeria. She is currently The Zeebah Foundation’s Director of Administration. She is also affiliated with Gradient Hill (Nig) Ltd. and Peter Bello Foundation.<\/p>\n

Board Secretary<\/p>\n

A cultural architect by craft, Glory Shaahu has a broad range of interests; one of which is influencing desirable attitude shifts in corporate spaces, as well as in individuals. An advocate of the unschooled approach to life, Glory infuses an unscripted and unconventional air into The Zeebah Foundation, where she serves as Secretary to the Board.<\/p>\n
