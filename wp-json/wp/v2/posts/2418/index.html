Excursion to a Supermarket in Abuja by The Zeebah Autism Center’s Children<\/h2>\n

It was all fun and learning as The Zeebah Place<\/a> children embarked on an excursion to the BMT African Gardens Abuja on the 22nd November 2019.<\/p>\n

It all started with a brief snack break after which they all proceeded to a train ride and tour of the garden.<\/p>\n

A stopover was made at the zoological area where the children with the help of a tour guide had an opportunity to see various animals such as; Ostriches, Baboons, Monkeys, horses, Crocodiles, geese, Hyenas, etc. Some of the children interacted with the Monkeys and Baboons by handing them snacks and offering handshakes.<\/p>\n

Lastly, the children made a final stop at the AMB. DR. BAMANGA M.TUKUR INTERNATIONAL LIBRARY. While in there, they got to see what a standard library looks like.<\/p>\n
