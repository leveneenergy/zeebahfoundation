The United Nation\u2019s 12th<\/sup> annual edition of the World Autism Awareness<\/a> Day holds on the 2nd<\/sup> of April,2019.<\/p>\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n

This event is internationally recognized and celebrated every year, to encourage the Member States of the United Nations to take measures and raise awareness about Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD)<\/a> throughout the world. It was enacted by the United Nations General Assembly resolution “62\/139.<\/p>\r\n

<\/p>\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n

Activities are planned every year to further increase knowledge of the challenges faced by children, individuals and their families.<\/p>\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n

The Zeebah Foundation\u2019s clinical director Dr. Julia Ejiogu<\/a> is billed to speak at this year\u2019s World Autism Awareness Day conference on 2nd<\/sup> April 2019 at the UN Headquarters in New York.<\/p>\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n

The theme of this year\u2019s conference is \u201cAssistive Technologies, Active Participation\u201d. Her panel will elaborate on \u201cCommunication: A Human Right\u201d.<\/p>\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n

This event can be viewed via live webcast on UN Web TV:\u00a0 http:\/\/webtv.un.org\/<\/a><\/p>\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n

Date: 2 April 2019<\/p>\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n
