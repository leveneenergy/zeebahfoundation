Parenting a child with autism<\/a> is one of the most difficult and confusing roles in life.<\/p>\n

It is sometimes easy to ignore warning signs of ASD<\/a>. Some parents may justify it as \u2018my child is still young, so let\u2019s just wait and see\u2019. Even worse, some parents may already have a gut feeling that something is wrong, but social pressure makes it difficult to accept this, and hence they avoid the issue.<\/p>\n

Since early intervention in autism<\/a>\u00a0is a critical part of success in autism therapy<\/a>, the earlier you seek professional diagnosis and advise, the earlier you can start<\/p>\n

Below are some advises on how you as a parent of an autistic child can help your child.<\/p>\n

    \n
  1. Be your child’s advocate: speak up for them always, ask questions on their behalf<\/li>\n
  2. Educate yourself on Autism, therapies, the latest trends in special education. You need to be able to discuss issues from a point of knowledge<\/li>\n
  3. Repetition helps: Continue practising skills learnt at therapy while at home. Make sure homework is done.<\/li>\n
  4. Take your child out to social events if possible: social skills need to be practised often.<\/li>\n
  5. Encourage exercise<\/a>, healthy eating habits and maintain good hydration<\/li>\n
  6. Report any changes at home to your therapist: in Behavior, environment, sleeping, eating. They all impact your child’s progress and ability to learn<\/li>\n
  7. Attend all meetings, training and follow up appointments with your therapist: you need to know what is going well and where improvements are needed<\/li>\n
  8. Plan your vacations around your child’s schedule: minimize interruptions. Remember the more aggressive and consistent the therapy and Instruction is the better the outcome.<\/li>\n
  9. Be patient: it’s a marathon, not a sprint. It’s all about practice and more practice.<\/li>\n
  10. Celebrate your child’s success no matter how small, it is important to look back at where you have come from.<\/li>\n<\/ol>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
